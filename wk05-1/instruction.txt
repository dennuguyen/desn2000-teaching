Workshop Activity

1. What were your personas' CNs?

2. Brainstorm/write a bunch of stories around your personas and CNs
	Stories have a setting
	Stories have a character
	Stories have a problem

3. Which of these stories are most emotionally and rationally compelling?
	Who do you feel most empathy to?
	Who has the realest problem?
	Is the story interesting/engaging?

4. Your story should follow this format:
	context/user -> problem -> solution
